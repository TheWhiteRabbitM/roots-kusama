01 / Problem

The oldest anti-Sybil system
in human history

As AI blurs the boundary between human and machine, current Proof of Personhood solutions are technically sophisticated but culturally thin they verify existence without verifying belonging.

In small Southern Italian villages, every family carries a 'u nomme 'e casa a house name, passed down orally for centuries. Invisible to registries. Incomprehensible to outsiders. Impossible for any AI to fake.

Pre-digital identity
House names are hyper-local, orally transmitted, semantically rich, socially validated and they grow organically as families branch across generations.
"You cannot fake belonging to a place if you do not know who the people are and what they are called."

03 / Architecture

Two layers, one truth

Off-Chain Layer
Oral Collection Sessions
Bars, homes, piazzas. 60+ house names with etymology, attribution, lineage.
The Book of Names
100-copy printed archive deposited with the municipality.
The Lineage Graph
Visual DAG of how names branch over time the conceptual model for on-chain structure.
On-Chain Layer Kusama
Kusama Identity Pallet
house_name · village_quarter · attestation_hash · lineage_parent. No custom contract.
ROOTS Token
Native assets pallet, transfer: None. Metadata on IPFS. roots_parent_token_id creates the DAG.
OpenGov Referendum
Gated vote only attested identities. Real community question, result to municipality.

The most unlikely
Kusama users imaginable

This experiment belongs on Kusama. The Identity Pallet, OpenGov, and native asset minting already exist we are not building new tooling. We are proving Kusama's primitives work for a context their designers never imagined.

07 / ROOTS NFT

A living portrait
of your place in the graph

Every attested family receives a generative NFT a unique visual artwork procedurally rendered from their position in the lineage graph. Each one evolves over time.

Trunk width
Proportional to lineage depth ancient families grow wider.
Branch count
Equals living child tokens. When a new house name is born, the parent NFT grows a new branch.
Color & animation
Terracotta (new) → deep amber (ancient). High-reputation families move slower, more majestic.
When a child token is minted with roots_parent_token_id, the NFT artwork re-renders with a new branch.
Disclaimer
ROOTS NFTs have zero monetary value. They cannot be traded or sold. Identity attestation records only.
